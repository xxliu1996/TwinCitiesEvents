# Twin Cities Events: July 23 – August 6, 2026

This week's roundup skews toward drag brunches, live music, and Aquatennial-adjacent fun in Minneapolis alongside a steady stream of low-key evening and weekend programming at Saint Paul Public Library branches (crafts, book clubs, yoga, tech help). Two drag brunches (Roxy's Cabaret and Bumbling Fools Mead) land the same Sunday, and the Cedar Cultural Center hosts an eclectic pair of shows — Sleepytime Gorilla Museum and the antifascist benefit hootenanny Conspiracy to Rock. If community and creativity are more your speed, there's a fashion pop-up, a paint & sip, karaoke night, and several free outdoor fitness classes (CorePower Yoga, lululemon Pilates). In total, 32 events made the cut after filtering out a handful of clearly kids-only library programs (a dog-reading session and several STEAM crafts for ages 7–11). It's a solid two weeks for anyone looking to mix nightlife with laid-back neighborhood happenings.
