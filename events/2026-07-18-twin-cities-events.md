# Twin Cities Events: July 18 – August 1, 2026

This week's pull turned up 33 adult-friendly events across the metro, running from tonight through Saturday, August 1. Saint Paul Public Library and Ramsey County Library branches dominate the calendar with low-key, drop-in programming — book clubs, yoga, crafting circles, tech help, and a karaoke night — while Eventbrite fills in the higher-energy options: a bottomless drag brunch, an outdoor CorePower Yoga class at the Walker ahead of Aquatennial weekend, a clean-energy networking reception, and a secondhand/upcycled fashion show. Book lovers are especially well served, with at least five distinct book clubs meeting across the two weeks. Weekday library events cluster in the 6–7 PM window as expected, while weekends open up with morning and early-afternoon options like Coffee and Crosswords and laser-engraving workshops. A handful of clearly kids-only library programs (FunLab, Paw Pals dog-reading) were manually dropped even though they technically passed the keyword filter, and a UK-based Eventbrite listing was correctly excluded by this week's script fix for out-of-state listings.
